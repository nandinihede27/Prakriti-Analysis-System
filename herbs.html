Ayurvedic Herbs

Short descriptions to get you started.

Ashwagandha

Adaptogenic herb that calms stress and supports energy. Often used to balance Vata & Kapha.

Turmeric

Anti-inflammatory, improves digestion and supports immunity.

Trikatu

Combination of black pepper, long pepper and ginger; supports digestion and metabolic fire (Agni).

Brahmi

Supports memory and calms the mind — helpful when Vata is high.
